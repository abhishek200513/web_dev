Our Distinctive Restaurants

From Michelin-starred haute cuisine to charming Parisian bistro fare, we cater to every palate.

L'Ambroisie

Michelin-Starred French Haute Cuisine

Experience culinary artistry at its finest. L'Ambroisie offers an intimate setting where traditional French techniques meet innovative presentation, celebrating seasonal ingredients with unparalleled elegance.

  • Dinner: Tuesday - Saturday, 7:00 PM - 10:30 PM
  • Dress Code: Formal

Le Petit Salon

Chic Parisian Bistro

A vibrant and relaxed atmosphere offering classic French bistro dishes with a modern twist. Perfect for a leisurely breakfast, casual lunch, or a delightful evening meal.

  • Breakfast: Daily, 7:00 AM - 10:30 AM
  • Lunch: Daily, 12:00 PM - 2:30 PM
  • Dinner: Daily, 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Le Bar Cochon

Elegant Cocktail Lounge

Unwind in our sophisticated bar, offering a curated selection of fine wines, champagnes, and expertly crafted cocktails. The perfect setting for a pre-dinner aperitif or a relaxed nightcap.

  • Open Daily: 5:00 PM - 1:00 AM
  • Live Piano Music: Select Evenings

Private Dining & Culinary Events

For intimate gatherings or grand celebrations, Le Grandeur offers exclusive private dining rooms designed with meticulous attention to detail. Our culinary team can craft bespoke menus to ensure your event is truly unforgettable.

We also host a calendar of unique culinary events, from wine tastings with acclaimed sommeliers to masterclasses with our Michelin-starred chefs. Inquire with our concierge for upcoming dates.
